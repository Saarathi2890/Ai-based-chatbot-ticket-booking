Highlights

  • The Indian Museum in Kolkata, India is one of the oldest and largest museums in the country.
  • It's known for its extensive collection of over 100,000 artifacts and art, including antiques, armor, ornaments, fossils, skeletons, mummies, and Mughal paintings.
  • The museum's collections depict India's cultural history from prehistoric times to the Muslim era, with detailed presentations that make every visit enriching.
  • The museum also has live performances, a restaurant, and a wheelchair accessible entrance.
