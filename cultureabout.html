About Us

The Ministry of Culture is responsible for the preservation and promotion of art and culture in India. The Ministry works towards conserving and promoting India's rich cultural heritage, and fostering the cultural expressions and creativity of all Indian citizens.

Our Vision

To make India proud of its rich and diverse cultural heritage, to promote cultural tourism, and to ensure that our culture is inclusive and accessible to all sections of society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a vibrant cultural atmosphere across the country by supporting cultural institutions, artists, and heritage preservation projects. We aim to make culture a key driver of economic growth, community development, and social integration.

What We Do

The Ministry of Culture administers numerous initiatives, schemes, and programs aimed at conserving and promoting India's tangible and intangible cultural heritage. We work closely with cultural institutions, NGOs, and state governments to preserve monuments, promote arts and crafts, and support artists and performers.
