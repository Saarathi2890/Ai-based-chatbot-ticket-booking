Highlights

The National Gallery of Modern Art in Delhi, India has a collection of more than 14,000 artworks, including works that are over 150 years old.

The gallery showcases the work of many artists, including Thomas Daniell, Abanindranath Tagore, Raja Ravi Verma, Gaganendranath Tagore, Rabindranath Tagore, and Nandalal Bose.
